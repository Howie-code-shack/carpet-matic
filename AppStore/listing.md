## Description
CarpetMatic turns room measurements into a carpet order and a cut plan — in the time it takes to walk back to the van.

Enter each room's width and length. CarpetMatic works out the strips, packs them onto your roll width (4 m, 5 m, or anything from 1–5 m), and tells you exactly how many linear metres to order — with strips from different rooms sharing roll length wherever they nest.

BUILT FOR FITTERS
• Rooms in, strips out — no fiddly piece-by-piece input
• Pile direction handled properly: arrows on every strip, tap to rotate, and a smart default that minimises waste
• Whole-roll layout view: see every cut, where it comes from, and what's left over
• Offcut shown in m² and % — know your waste before you order
• Pattern repeat support: cuts round up to the pattern boundary automatically
• Stair calculator: steps × (tread + riser + nosing) → done
• Underlay m² and gripper metres on every job
• Price per metre → instant estimate for the customer

HAND THE CUSTOMER A PROPER JOB SHEET
Export a PDF with your business name and number on it: total metres, cut list per room with pile arrows, offcut, underlay, gripper, and the estimate.

WORKS WHERE YOU WORK
Everything calculates on the phone — no signal needed. Projects sync automatically between your iPhone and Mac through iCloud. Metric-first: metres, centimetres, and real UK roll widths.

FAIR PRICING
Your first project is free, full-featured on screen. One one-off purchase unlocks unlimited projects and PDF export. No subscription. No per-job fees.
